The journey of Apex began with the launch of its tannery unit in 1976 and later carved its way into shoemaking with Apex Footwear Ltd. in 1990. With that began a journey of excellence to create fine-quality leather shoes for the world. Born from the inspiration of leather craftsmanship and paving its way in the industry as a pioneer, creatively crafting Italian shoemaking artistry into its designs. Through the years, Apex has mastered the art of shoemaking, using innovation in construction to create the perfect pair.
